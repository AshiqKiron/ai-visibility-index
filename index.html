About the Research

We systematically track how ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity cite sources over time. Our metrics reveal which brands are gaining visibility and which are suffering from algorithmic instability.

Key Metrics:

  • Flicker Rate: How often the #1 source changes daily.
  • Citation Stability Index (CSI): Long-term reliability of a source.
  • Prompt Sensitivity: How much wording changes the results.
